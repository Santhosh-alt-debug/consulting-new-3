FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan after starting?
Yes. You can upgrade or adjust at any renewal point. We'll pro-rate the difference and brief your team on scope changes.
What happens after the minimum term?
Engagements move to a rolling monthly arrangement. Continue, scale, or exit with 30 days' notice — no lock-ins.
Do you work with startups and SMEs?
Absolutely. Our Starter plan is specifically designed for growth-stage companies. We also offer project-based engagements.
Is the consulting team dedicated to my business?
Growth and Enterprise clients receive a named lead consultant who manages a team around your specific needs.
What's the onboarding process?
We start with a 2-hour discovery session, followed by a diagnostic deep-dive. By end of week two, you have a clear roadmap.
Do you offer project-based pricing?
Yes. For defined-scope projects like market entry studies or M&A due diligence, we offer fixed-fee pricing.
