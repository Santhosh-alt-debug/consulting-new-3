OUR STORY

From Salem to the
Global Stage

Stackly was founded in 2018 in Salem, Tamil Nadu, with a single conviction: world-class consulting should not be the exclusive privilege of global metropolises.

Today, Stackly operates across 18 countries, has advised over 340 enterprises, and is recognised as one of South Asia's fastest-growing consulting firms.

We believe the best consultants embed, challenge, co-create, and see results through to the very end.
